Long Islands' Southcoast Fishing Guide!




This map shows my favorite fishing locations along with dock locations where you can find trip rentals. To go along with the boat trip rentals I included a water feature map that highlights some obscure artificial reef locations and provides information on the substrate such as rocky bottoms or sandy bottoms. The goal of this map is to help find non-crowded and beautiful fishing locations along with Long Islands' southern coastline. This map is important to me because of its locations that I found while looking for cool fishing spots with my family and friends. Over many years my knowledge of the best locations grew and eventually I compiled thousands of hours fishing into a handful of locations including Captree state park and more obscure public docks. I am still relatively new to fishing and I am always looking to find new places to visit that are not crowded, but still offer amazing scenery, easy access, and can be seen as a personal fishing area. Long Island has some of the best locations for coastline fishing and offers a large array of species that always offer both amateur and expert fishermen an opportunity to just enjoy the hobby. I only recently began exploring the off-coast fishing experience, but I have already identified some of the lesser-known artificial reefs that were created over the years that are not always posted on digital maps currently available. The knowledge is mostly by word of mouth by local fishermen who frequent these areas using their sonar devices to find the best locations themselves, and I think it would be amazing if knowledge of Long Islands surrounding water features could be available in a more accessible and easy to use digital resource. Eventually, if someone like me was interested in charter a small boat to go fishing you can just tell your captain exactly where you'd like to go! Instead of these features slowly fading into obscurity and only being found on historical maps or other hard to find sources it can be compiled into a master map with all of these amazing off-shore features consolidated into an easy website or maybe a phone application.
